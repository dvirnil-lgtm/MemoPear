==============================================================================
PRODUCTION NOTES
==============================================================================
- Record app footage on a demo/QA account on a free trial so nothing is paywalled.
- Pre-load 3–5 realistic sample contacts before filming the Contacts list.
- Have a sample badge QR code and a business-card image ready for the scan shots.
- Shoot app screens in a narrow / portrait frame (mobile-first layout).
- B-roll to capture or license: busy expo hall, booth conversations, handshake,
  badge close-ups, a messy stack of business cards (the "problem" shot).
- Keep app interactions slow and deliberate; leave ~1s after each tap for cuts.
- Superimpose on-screen TEXT as bold, short kinetic captions (brand teal on navy).
- Always end on the logo lockup + CTA + price chip.

DISCLAIMER TO VERIFY BEFORE PUBLISHING:
- Confirm the current price ($2.80/month) and any trial length on screen/in VO.
- "Auto-deletes after 30 days" is a real product behavior — keep it accurate.
