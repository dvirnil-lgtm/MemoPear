# MemoPear — "How To" Video Guide (Script & Storyboard)

A ready-to-record walkthrough of MemoPear. Target length **~4–5 minutes**.
Each scene lists the **screen**, the **on-screen action** (what to click/show),
the **voiceover (VO)**, and **on-screen text** callouts. Cut any section you
don't need — scenes are modular.

> Tip: record at a phone-portrait aspect (MemoPear is mobile-first) or a narrow
> browser window so the UI looks native. Use a demo account on a free trial so
> every feature is unlocked.

---

## Scene 0 — Intro / Hook (0:00–0:15)

- **Screen:** MemoPear home page (logo + hero).
- **Action:** Slow zoom on the logo, then cut to the Add-Contact screen.
- **VO:** "Meet MemoPear — the fastest way to capture and follow up on the
  contacts you meet at conferences. Scan a badge, snap a card, save the details,
  and they're on every device you own. Let's take a quick tour."
- **On-screen text:** `MemoPear — Simply Better Lead Collection`

---

## Scene 1 — Sign up & free trial (0:15–0:45)

- **Screen:** Login page (`/login`).
- **Action:**
  1. Click **Sign Up**.
  2. Show the three options: **Continue with Google**, **Continue with
     LinkedIn**, or **email + password**.
  3. Complete sign-up → app lands on the **Add a Contact** screen.
- **VO:** "Create an account with Google, LinkedIn, or email. Every new account
  starts on a free trial, so you can try everything right away. Because your
  account lives in the cloud, you can sign in from your phone, laptop, or a
  teammate's device and pick up right where you left off."
- **On-screen text:** `Free trial • Sign in from any device`

> First-time users see a quick guided tour automatically. You can replay it any
> time in your first week with the **❓** button (mention this in Scene 7).

---

## Scene 2 — Capture a contact: QR badge & business card (0:45–1:45)

- **Screen:** Add a Contact form (`/gather`).
- **Action:**
  1. Tap the **QR** button → scan a conference badge QR code → watch the name,
     company, email and phone auto-fill.
  2. Clear it, then tap the **📸 card** button → take/upload a photo of a
     business card → the AI reads every field and fills the form.
- **VO:** "Capturing a contact takes seconds. Tap **QR** to scan a badge — the
  name, company, and contact details fill in instantly. Or tap the **card**
  button to photograph a business card, and our AI reads every field for you.
  No typing required."
- **On-screen text:** `QR badge scan` → `AI business-card scan`

---

## Scene 3 — Edit details & choose follow-up channels (1:45–2:20)

- **Screen:** Add a Contact form, fields populated.
- **Action:**
  1. Fix/typo-correct a field (e.g., job title) by hand.
  2. Under **"Best way to follow up,"** tap a few channels (LinkedIn, Email,
     WhatsApp) → show the handle inputs appear → fill one in.
  3. Type a quick note in the **notes** box ("Met at booth 12 — wants a demo").
- **VO:** "Anything the scan missed, just type it in. Then pick how you'll follow
  up — LinkedIn, email, WhatsApp, and more — and add a quick note so you remember
  the conversation later."
- **On-screen text:** `Pick your follow-up channels` · `Add a note`

---

## Scene 4 — Save the contact (2:20–2:40)

- **Screen:** Add a Contact form.
- **Action:** Tap **Save Contact** → the **"Contact Saved ✅"** confirmation
  pops up briefly, the form clears, ready for the next person.
- **VO:** "Hit **Save Contact** — you'll see a quick confirmation, and the form
  resets so you're ready for the next person. Your contact is saved to your
  account and synced to all your devices automatically."
- **On-screen text:** `Saved & synced everywhere`

---

## Scene 5 — Your Contacts: review, enrich, follow up (2:40–3:30)

- **Screen:** **Contacts** tab (`/pipeline`).
- **Action:**
  1. Open the **Contacts** tab → show the list of saved contacts.
  2. Tap a contact to expand → show details and notes.
  3. Demonstrate **LinkedIn lookup** (one tap opens their LinkedIn search).
  4. Demonstrate **Generate follow-up email** → an AI-drafted email appears.
  5. Tap **Edit** to update a contact.
- **VO:** "Open the **Contacts** tab to see everyone you've saved. Tap anyone to
  see their details, look them up on LinkedIn, or generate a personalized
  follow-up email with one tap. Need to fix something? Just edit it inline."
- **On-screen text:** `LinkedIn lookup` · `AI follow-up emails`

---

## Scene 6 — Export & email your leads (3:30–4:00)

- **Screen:** Contacts tab with the selection bar visible.
- **Action:**
  1. Select a few contacts (the bulk action bar appears at the top).
  2. Tap **Export to Google Sheets** → name the sheet → it opens in a new tab.
  3. Back in MemoPear, tap **Email My Leads** → enter an address → it opens your
     email app pre-filled.
- **VO:** "Ready to act on your leads? Select contacts and push them straight to
  a new **Google Sheet**, or email your whole list to yourself or your CRM in
  one tap."
- **On-screen text:** `Export to Google Sheets` · `Email My Leads`

---

## Scene 7 — Team & seats (4:00–4:35)  *(optional — multi-seat plans)*

- **Screen:** **Team** tab (`/team`).
- **Action:**
  1. Show the **invite link** → copy it.
  2. Explain seats: the owner holds one seat; teammates join via the link.
  3. Show the list of members; demo **regenerate invite link** and **remove a
     member**.
- **VO:** "On a team plan, invite teammates by sharing one link — they sign in
  and instantly get full access under your subscription. Manage seats, reset the
  invite link, or remove someone any time from the **Team** tab. And signing in
  from a new device shows you the same team and the same access, everywhere."
- **On-screen text:** `Share one invite link` · `Same access on every device`

---

## Scene 8 — Profile, privacy & retention (4:35–5:00)

- **Screen:** **Profile** tab.
- **Action:**
  1. Show editing **name** and **conferences you're attending**.
  2. Show billing / **manage subscription**.
  3. Briefly show the **privacy/retention** notice.
- **VO:** "In **Profile**, set your name and the conferences you're attending so
  contacts stay organized, and manage your billing. One thing to know about
  privacy: your contacts never leave your account, and for security we
  automatically delete each contact 30 days after it's created — so export
  anything you want to keep."
- **On-screen text:** `Contacts auto-delete after 30 days — export to keep`

## Recording tips
- Use a **demo account on a free trial** (or a QA account) so every feature is
  unlocked and no paywall interrupts the flow.
- Pre-load **2–3 sample contacts** before recording Scene 5/6 so lists look real.
- Have a **sample badge QR code** and a **business card image** ready for Scene 2.
- Keep cursor movements slow and deliberate; pause ~1s after each click so the
  editor has room to cut.
- If recording on desktop, use a narrow window (~420px wide) to match the
  mobile-first layout.
